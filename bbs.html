Bachelor of Business Studies(BBS)
It offers a strong foundation in allied areas of business such as language, economic analysis, legal environment and quantitative method to prepare graduates to understand, analyze and comprehend the management concepts, theories and practices along with core business studies encompassing and integrating all functional areas to provide graduates with and appreciation of the diversity and inter- relationship of business and management issues. To provide graduates with some degree of functional expertise, this course also provides an opportunity to concentrate in one area of specialization such as accounting, finance, human resources, management and marketing.
